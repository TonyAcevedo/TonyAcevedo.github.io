How do I install SBC Yahoo! Parental Controls?
You must install SBC Yahoo! Parental Controls on all computers on which you would like to restrict access to the Internet. To install, follow these steps:
  1. Go to ypc.yahoo.com/edit to initialize your settings.
    By default all settings are set to "off."
  2. Open the "Start" menu on your computer and select "Programs."
  3. Select "SBC Yahoo!."
  4. Click "Activate SBC Yahoo! Parental Controls."
  5. Follow the instructions during the installation.
  6. Restart your computer.
  7. Visit ypc.yahoo.com/edit to change your settings.

After You Finish
Use the Parental Controls icon in the system tray to log in or out and to temporarily disable SBC Yahoo! Parental Controls.
