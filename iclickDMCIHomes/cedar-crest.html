DISTINCT FEATURES

A Touch of Neo-Asian Minimalist Design
The development blends modern, Asian-inspired aesthetics and a minimalist design philosophy, to give you a residential haven that’s serene and private.

An Idyllic Community That Nurtures
The project features a central amenity area with a clubhouse that opens up to surrounding views and amenities, conducive to family bonding and relaxation. Likewise enjoy worry-free services and convenient facilities. 
Nearness to Everything That Matters to You
Strategically located in the progressive city of Taguig, it is minutes away from the Makati Central Business District and Bonifacio Global City, the Ortigas CBD, and Quezon City in the North. Places in Southern Manila, like Paranaque and Alabang, are easily accessible through the C-5 Road.

Inspiring the Peace of Mind You Deserve
Trained professionals of DMCI Homes' Property Management Team are on hand to extend assistance to all residents, ensuring safety and peace of mind. Property Management services include maintenance of facilities, garbage disposal system, 24-hour security, perimeter fence, and facilities at the clubhouse. 

A Heritage of Building Excellence
Because the project is built and developed by DMCI Homes, you are assured of getting the best value for your money, exceptional features and amenities, and a world-standard level of craftsmanship borne out of more than 50 years’ worth of experience in the construction and development industry.

HOW TO GET THERE?
Via C5(From Libis/Ortigas Ave.): Take C5 south bound route, drive straight ahead passing thru Tiendesitas/SM Pasig, C5 Kalayaan and Market! Market! Landmarks. Continue moving ahead, then exit to Bayani Rd. tunnel (near Heritage Park) and take a U-Turn going C5 northbound, drive straight ahead upon reaching BCDA/Petron, turn right to P. Diego Silang Road, follow the path, then turn right to Levi Mariano ave., drive straight ahead, and finally turn right to Acacia Lane/ Rosewood Pointe , drive straight and turn left to Cedar Crest.

Via Edsa (Coming from SLEX): Take Edsa north bound route, turn right to McKinley Rd., passing thru Manila Polo Club/Manila Golf Club, and upon reaching Bonifacio Global City, turn right leading to Lawton Ave., drive straight ahead and turn left to Bayani road(Heritage park), continue moving straight ahead passing thru the tunnel(C5) then turn left going C5 north bound, drive straight ahead upon reaching BCDA/Petron, turn right to P. Diego Silang Road, follow the path, then turn right to Levi Mariano ave., move shortly straight ahead, and finally turn right to Acacia Lane /Rosewood Pointe , drive straight and turn left to Cedar Crest.
