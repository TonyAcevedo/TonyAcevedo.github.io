The units in the remaining two towers of DMCI Homes’ Tivoli Garden Residences are set for turn over in March and September this year. Located in Mandaluyong City, Iris tower is already 100% completed, while Hibiscus Tower is already topped.

Young families and professionals seeking contemporary urban living solutions in a resort-inspired environment are encouraged to take advantage of the last few residential units in Tivoli Garden Residences. The first three towers, Bauhinia, Heliconia, and Eugenia, have been sold out since 2010.

The middle-income property market has started to embrace the concept of enjoying year-round staycation in a condominium home in the city. DMCI Homes, a company of top-rate builders and engineers, pioneered themed condominium projects for young families in need of inspiring homes and spaces. In Tivoli Garden Residences’IrisTower, one of the architectural highlights is The Observatory, a landscaped promenade with sitting areas that provides residents and their guests a private, scenic view of the metro skyline from the roof deck. At the perch of Hibiscus is the landscaped Sky park, perfect for al fresco lounging under the stars or for looking out over the city’s lights below. The two towers share podium deck amenities such as a badminton court, basketball court and leisure deck.

Following the principle that buying a home is more about investing in a better way of living, Tivoli Garden Residences provides solid proof of the company’s dedication to developing innovative residential units and spaces with world-class craftsmanship.  The 42-storey-high Iris tower is engineered with the company’s hallmark Lumiventt Design Technology that welcomes optimal light and airflow into the structures, helping unit owners save on electricity costs. The developer achieved this by building sky patios every five floors, which are 3-storey-high openings on the side of the tower. Completing this energy efficient design are single-loaded hallways and breezeways on the left and right wings of every level, as well central garden atriums that bring the relaxing presence of nature indoors. A vibrant mix of community amenities helps achieve the overall experience of vacation and wellness.

On track to meet construction and delivery targets for Tivoli Garden Residences, DMCI Homes is close to selling out the remaining studio, 2-bedroom, and 3-bedroom units at Iris and Hibiscus towers. (Article from DMCI Homes Corporate News)
