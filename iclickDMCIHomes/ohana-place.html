Inspired the relaxed life in the tropical paradise of Hawaii, OHANA PLACE brings together Modern Polunesian architecture, lush tropical greenery, a wide range of resort-like amenities and the conveniences of professional property management enveloped in one community within the progressive city of Las Piñas. Named after the Hawaiian word for family, OHANA PLACE offers the distinct ambience of Hawaii in the comforts of your home.  

DISTINCT FEATURES

Architectural Glories of the Islands
Steeply-inclined roofs, lightly-toned woods, exotic artwork and floral embellishments – physical articulations of Modern Polynesian architecture that is the chosen inspiration for this development. Ohana Place is a micropolis exploding in expression and colors – a true feast for the senses. We are amplifying the idyllic lifestyle of this wonderful culture through the careful planning and layout of the residential buildings and open spaces within the development.

Dedicated to Your Enjoyment
More than 6,600 sqm. of land has been dedicated just for amenities. It boasts of a Hawaiian-inspired outdoor recreational area that is highlighted by an expansive pool complex made more impressive by a rock formation surrounded by water cascades and slides. Featured also are cabanas with grill pits, active play areas like basketball court and children’s playground and pockets parks and gardens teeming with exotic flora. At the center is the Luau Pavilion, the Grand Clubhouse of Ohana Place, equipped with a fitness gym, entertainment room and multi-purpose halls. Here, family fun is just outside your doorsteps.

HOW TO GET THERE?

Via SLEX( From Makati)
Exit at the Alabang Interchange, then take the Alabang- Zapote road heading towards the Las Pinas City . Drive straight passing by northgate and the Alabang Town Center . Shortly after Toyota -Alabang , right along the national road you’ll find Ohana Place .

Via Las Piñas national road ( From Zapote )
drive straight along Alabang- Zapote road passing by the Municipal Hall of Las Piñas City and the University of Perpetual Help a few meters after SM Southmall , right along the national road , you’ll find Ohana Place.
