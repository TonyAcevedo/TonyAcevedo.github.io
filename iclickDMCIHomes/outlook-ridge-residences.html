Outlook Ridge Residences is a mountain-resort-style home only for the select few.

It has a climate that is cooler by 8 degrees Celsius than any parts of the country, making it the perfect escape from the searing heat of Manila. Here, a cup of steaming coffee is more soothing as you take a sip of it while gazing at the scenic view of the mountain vista.

Outdoor Living
