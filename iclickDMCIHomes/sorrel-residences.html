DISTINCT FEATURES

Move freely within the city
Strategically located in Sociego Street in Sampaloc, Manila, going to and from Sorrel Residences is easy. It’s only walking distance from Aurora-V.MAPA LRT2 Station from shopping centers and business districts of Manila, Mandaluyong, and Quezon City. It is close to reputable schools and public transportations are also close by. 
Feel free to move about
As in other condominium projects of DMCI Homes, Sorrel Residences DMCI Sta Mesa Manila will have a high footprint-to-building space ratio, with a major percentage of the entire land area allotted to open spaces with lots of greens to showcase a more nature-friendly environment and different lifestyle amenities to encourage a relaxed, recreational lifestyle. The park-like atmosphere allows everyone to feel in touch with the outdoors on a daily basis. 
Strategic Location and City Skyline Views
Found at the heart of the city, it is close to all landmark cultural, commercial, business, and educational hubs in the metro.

A Worthwhile Investment
Centrally located, the travel time from home to commercial and lifestyle hubs or business establishments is minimized, allowing you to save on gas mileage. Its proximity to schools provides the option to rent out units to students or professionals working in the immediate area. 
Efficient Property Management
Within a Secure Environment to free residents from everyday worries, a property management team shall oversee the management of daily services, and the upkeep and maintenance of perimeter grounds and building features.

Real Smart Living
Because it’s built by the country’s first Triple A builder/developer, you are assured of secure, high quality home in a fully-serviced resort-like community managed by a team of professionals.
