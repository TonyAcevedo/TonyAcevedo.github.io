Zinnia Residences Munoz Quezon CIty Since the norm in urban living is now the condo residence, DMCI Homes, the residential resort specialist, maintains that the middle-income market of young families also deserve a home where they can live everyday within a nurturing environment – a place where they can experience comfortable living after confining, competitive hours at work. Backed by a construction heritage of more than 55 years, the triple-A builder/developer continues its objective to provide a wide variety of residential projects designed for young professionals and start-up families with modest income and discerning tastes.  

DISTINCT FEATURES

Zinnia Residences Munoz Quezon CIty development will consist of 3 high-rise buildings of 30 levels each in a total land area of 19,493 sqms. The project will offer Studio, 1BR and 2BR unit options with a total of 1,396 units.  
Like all other residential developments by DMCI Homes,Zinnia Residences Munoz Quezon CIty will be a master-planned resort-themed community with first-rate features, nature-rich setting, wide selection of country club-style amenities, well-secured community, convenient location plus an impressive lineup of property management services.  

LOCATION

Developer: DMCI Homes
Location: 1211 EDSA near Munoz, Quezon CIty
Land Area: 19,493 sqm
Floor Area: 25.05 – 53.02 sqm
Unit Types: Studio, 1BR, 2BR

Zinnia Towers (or Zinnia Residences) will be developed by DMCI Homes along 1211 EDSA, across Wilcon Builders, in Munoz, Quezon City, Metro Manila, Philippines.
As the site is along EDSA, a major thouroughfare where buses, taxi cabs and private vehicles ply, transportation is guaranteed 24 hours a day.  Zinnia Towers DMCI Munoz Quezon CIty is also transit-oriented as it is just a short distance away from LRT-MRT Loop – Roosevelt Station, which connects to other transit systems plying the whole of Manila making the place even more convenient for commuters.

Young professionals and executives with offices in Makati will value the availability of transportation and major thoroughfares that link them directly to the central business district. Leisure-seekers will also find Zinnia Towers Munoz QC by DMCI Homes a haven, being located near malls such as SM North Edsa, The Block and Trinoma that offer dining and clubbing choices for people who need to unwind. The ideal location of the site gives residents and their families the comfort of having their needs within easy reach through proximity to various leisure and recreation spots, retail establishments, public markets, hospitals, educational institutions, and places of worship.
